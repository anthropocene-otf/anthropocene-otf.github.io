Anthropocene.otf aims to facilitate a proactive comparison with the themes of climate change and political participation Anthropocene.otf is a parametric typeface which, distributed free of charge and linked to an air quality index will change its design based on ait quality: those who want tho change the parameters of the character, will have to do it in the real world, deciding to reduce their carbon footprint or promoting virtous behavior thanks to the use of this character
