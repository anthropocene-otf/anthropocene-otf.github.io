Anthropocene.otf aims to facilitate a proactive comparison regarding the themes of climate change and political participation.
Anthropocene.otf is a parametric typeface which, distributed free of charge and linked to an air quality index, will change its design dynamically according to the current air pollutants in your area.
Those who want to change the character, will have to do it in the real world, deciding to reduce their carbon footprint or promoting virtous behaviour.
