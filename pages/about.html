About the project

Anthropocene.otf was developed for the biennal Federico Alessandri Prize I edition of 2019, a call organized through the collaboration between the Academy of Fine Arts and the I.S.I.A. of Urbino in memory of the talented and beloved student Federico.
The authors of Anthropocene.otf are Michelangelo Alesi, Elia Chiarucci, Gianluca Ciancaglini and Amedeo Mencarini.
