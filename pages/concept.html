The concept

The core of this project is to develop a parametric font that, in its nature, was modified by the concept that made us create it: the impact of human carbon footprint.
The font changes the way it's seen dinamically thanks to an AQI of the nearest station control of the area where you're connecting from.

What is an AQI?

AQI stands for Air Quality Index and is used for daily reports of air quality by the connected stations around the world. The AQI is an average of various pollutants that are in the air and, if keept on a growing level, can become very hazardous for the humans biological functions.

We are currently using the aqicn.org data

How does this font reflects the AQI in your area?

The AQI range goes from 0 to 300, you can learn more about it here.
The lower the AQI value, the bolder the typeface; otherwise, the higher the AQI value, the thinner it will be.
