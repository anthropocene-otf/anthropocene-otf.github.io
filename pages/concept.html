The concept

The core of this project is to develop a parametric font modified, in its nature, by the concept that inspired us to create it in the first place: the impact of humansâ€™ carbon footprint on the environment.
This font dynamically changes its look following the AQI value of your nearest control station.

What is an AQI?

AQI stands for Air Quality Index and it is used in daily reports of air quality by all active control stations dislocated around the world.
The AQI is an average of various pollutants residing in the atmosphere; on higher values, it represents serious concerns and health hazards to human biological functions.

We are currently using the aqicn.org data

How does this font reflects the AQI in your area?

The AQI value range span is 0-300 (you can learn more about it here), and this typeface is a visual, straightforward representation of it.
The lower the AQI value, the bolder the typeface becomes; conversely, it will get thinner and thinner the more the AQI value grows.
